On the Program: A Gender Equality Initiative and Professional Visibility at Academic Conferences

with Marina Gertsberg, Esad Smajlbegovic, Daniel Urban, and Patrick Verwijmeren

Revise and Resubmit, Research Policy

Abstract: Persistent gender disparities characterize scientific careers. We study a gender equality initiative implemented at a leading academic conference, a key stage where visibility, reputation, and professional networks are shaped. Using a difference-in-differences design comparing two major conferences within the same discipline, we show that a time-limited initiative encouraging the appointment of women as paper discussants led to a sharp but temporary increase in female representation in this prestigious role. The increase did not come at the expense of quality. However, the initiative primarily affected already well-established women, with more limited gains for junior scholars.
